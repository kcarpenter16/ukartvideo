How to attend Videos for a Stadium:

1. Park for free in the Commonwealth Stadium Green Lot.

2. Follow signs to the Recruiting Room.

3. Show your ticket or buy one at the entrance.

4. Enjoy food and drinks at the concession stand (hot dogs, pretzels, popcorn, nachos, ice cream, candy bars, soft drinks, bottled water, domestic beer).

5. Get pumped up with a performance by the UK Cheerleaders.

6. Take your seat in the End Zone.

7. Listen to Carl Nathe announce the artists.

8. Watch and enjoy on the big screen videos by:

Paul Stephen Benjamin, Janet Biggs Victoria Fu, Brett Kashmere, Anya Liftig, Lilly McElroy, Didier Morelli, Michael David Murphy, Alix Pearlstein, Becky Sellinger, Joe Sola, and Scott Stark.
